# CityPulse Tweet Thread

## Tweet 1 — Hook

Current navigation updates traffic every few minutes using aggregated phone data.

But there are 40,000 municipal vehicles on Istanbul's roads RIGHT NOW — buses, ambulances, garbage trucks — producing ground-truth speed data every second.

That data is being wasted. Until now.

## Tweet 2 — What is CityPulse

CityPulse puts an x402 paywall on municipal fleet telemetry.

Pay 0.0003 USDC → get a route based on real-time ground truth from municipal vehicles on your corridor.

@ArcInternet instant finality = payment + route in seconds.
@circle USDC native gas = sub-cent payments that actually work.

## Tweet 3 — On-Demand Re-routing

Here's what no navigation app gives you: on-demand re-routing with real-time data.

30-minute drive? Query 5 times for under a penny. Each query = that exact second's ground truth from municipal vehicles.

You decide when to re-route. Not the app.

## Tweet 4 — Everyone Wins

The flywheel:

Driver pays → saves 7 min → gets home earlier
Municipality earns → from data that cost $0 to produce
Traffic distributes → buses faster, ambulances arrive sooner
Less congestion → less fuel → less imports → less pollution

Everyone wins. The municipality earns revenue while improving its own operations.

## Tweet 5 — Two Interfaces

Two interfaces, one protocol:

/drive — Navigation. Search destination, see standard route for free, pay sub-cent USDC to unlock the faster CityPulse route, navigate turn-by-turn.

/dashboard — Municipality panel. Real-time revenue, vehicle tracking, zone congestion, on-chain payment feed.
